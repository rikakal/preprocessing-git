##Additional Notes
Enclosed in this zip file the shapefile for Michigan's Congressional Districts ("Chestnut.shp") and supporting files, a PDF of compactness statistics ("Chestnut Compactness.pdf"), an Excel report of demographic summary statistics by district ("Chestnut matrix.xlsx"), and an Excel report of partisan fairness by district ("Partisan Fairness_Chestnut.xlsx").
